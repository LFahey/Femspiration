About Us

A little bit of information on the women behind Femspiration

Christina Macleod Mechanical Engineering whiz and general all-round top lass

Who am I? Christina Macleod

Where am I from? Canada

What field am I in? Studying towards BSc (Hons) Mechanical Engineering at the University of Edinburgh.

Why am I coding? After attending the Women in Technology Conference in Edinburgh in 2018, I was inspired by all of the fascinating women who spoke about their experiences in technology. Although each of them had different backgrounds in technology, they all shared one common trait, their passion for technology and getting more women involved in the field. One of the women at the conference spoke about helping run CodeFirst:Girls, and as soon as I look further into the programme, I knew that I had to apply for it.

Female role models? Ellen Stofan, Nathalia Rus, Kelly Spearman, Lydia Hallie, Women in STEM committee, Jacqueline Sotradis
